*Automated Draft Email Response — Legal Vendor Management Shared Mailbox.
Early Release
The Automated Draft Email Response solution is an AI-assisted workflow built for the Legal Operations Financial Vendor Management (FVM) team. It automates the process of responding to recurring questions received through the LillyLegalVendorManagement@lilly.com shared mailbox — primarily from law firms. When an email arrives, Outlook categorization rules automatically tag it based on subject, sender, or keywords. Power Automate picks it up, checks if it falls within an in-scope category, and if so, passes the email content to a Cortex AI assistant (Claude Sonnet 4.5), which generates a draft reply. That draft is then pre-populated in Outlook, addressed to the original sender, and queued for a team member to review, edit if needed, and send. No email is ever sent automatically — human review is a required step in the flow. Out-of-scope emails bypass the automation entirely and are handled manually. The key benefits are reduced time spent on routine responses, more consistent answers across the team, and a built-in audit trail through the human review step.
