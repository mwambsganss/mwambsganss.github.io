AI Stack at Lilly
The AI Stack at Lilly simplifies AI development with reusable, modular artificial intelligence (AI) components that can be easily integrated into various applications.
By leveraging reusable AI components, developers can save time and resources, ensuring consistency and reliability across projects. This approach accelerates innovation, as it allows teams to build complex AI systems efficiently without starting from scratch for each new project.

Table Generator
AI Platform
Table Generator uses LLMs to convert unstructured text or data (CSV/Excel) into editable tables, enabling complex tasks through natural language. It includes intent recognition, guardrails, and API access for seamless usability and security.

Text Comparator
AI Platform
Text Comparator is a versatile component that compares two input texts. It supports user-defined instructions, additional prompts, and various models, including GPT-4 and other large language models (LLMs).

Metadata Extractor
AI Platform
The Metadata Extraction module extracts structured information from text or PDF documents. Users can provide their own schema or have one generated automatically. The extracted metadata can be used independently or for downstream tasks (e.g., RAG).
