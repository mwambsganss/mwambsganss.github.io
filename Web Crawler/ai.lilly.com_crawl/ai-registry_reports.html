Reports

The following AI Registry Reports are available: 

  • Portfolio: High-level listing and summaries of submitted AI Registry items. Available to everyone with Lilly credentials. 

  • Operational: Metrics and details intended for review teams only. Most users will not have access to these reports. 

  • Tech Pipeline: Detailed metrics on the number of ideas and innovations in each stage of the Tech Innovation Pipeline. Available to all users with Lilly credentials. 

For access beyond Portfolio Reports, submit a service request through the Advanced Intelligence (AI) Account Request. If approved, access will be granted within 24 hours.
 
Note: Operational Report access is limited to designated review team members, and most requests will not be approved.
