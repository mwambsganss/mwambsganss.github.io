2025 AI UPSKILLING FOR TECH@LILLY LEADERS
GOAL: DEVELOP CORE SKILLS IN AI, ML, AND GENERATIVE AI TO STRENGTHEN TECHNICAL LEADERSHIP AND ACCELERATE THE DEVELOPMENT OF INNOVATIVE AI-DRIVEN SOLUTIONS, ULTIMATELY ADVANCING GROWTH AND IMPROVING PATIENT OUTCOMES.
Tech@Lilly leaders (M+, P5+, R7+) must complete the Tech@Lilly AI Certification Learning Journey Program for Leaders in SuccessFactors by
August 31, 2025
.

Frequently Asked Questions
What is the AI Ascend program?
expand accordion
The AI Ascend program is designed to enhance knowledge and capabilities across the organization. In 2025, it will focus on AI upskilling within Tech@Lilly.
What is the purpose of the AI certification for leaders?
expand accordion
The AI certification for leaders aims to strengthen technical leadership and accelerate the development of innovative AI-driven solutions, ultimately advancing growth and improving patient outcomes.
Who is required to complete the AI certification?
expand accordion
Leaders at M+, P5+, and R7+ levels must complete one of the specified AI certifications by July 30, 2025.
What are the available AI certifications for leaders?
expand accordion
Leaders can choose between the
Microsoft Azure AI Fundamentals (AI-900)
or the
AWS Certified AI Practitioner (AF-C01)
certifications, with the exception of cybersecurity leaders, who can choose between the
Microsoft Azure AI Fundamentals (AI-900)
, the
AWS Certified AI Practitioner (AF-C01)
certifications, or the
Certified AI Security Fundamentals (CAISF)
.
When is the deadline for completing the AI certification?
expand accordion
The deadline for Tech@Lilly leaders to complete the AI certification is August 31, 2025. This deadline is earlier than last year’s Cloud Fundamentals Certification deadline. Completion of the certification will be reviewed during August Performance Management check-ins.
When will the AI certification completion be reviewed?
expand accordion
The completion of the AI certification will be reviewed during the August 2025 Performance Management check-ins.
What happens when a new hire joins Tech@Lilly between now and August 31?
expand accordion
If a new Tech@Lilly employee is onboarded who meets the assignment criteria, they will get the AI Certification assigned to their training plan. They will have 158 days from their hire/start date to complete the certification.
How do we pay for the certification exam?
expand accordion
You should use your Lilly corporate credit card to pay for the exam and submit for reimbursement.
Can anyone in Tech@Lilly take the AI Certification, or is it only reimbursable for leaders?
expand accordion
Anyone in Tech@Lilly can take the AI Certification with management approval.
Should I get the Microsoft Cloud certification before working on Microsoft AI?
expand accordion
Cloud certification isn’t required, but it can help familiarize you with Azure services.
Is it better to stick with the same cloud platform for AI certification as the foundational certification?
expand accordion
It’s not necessary to stick with the same cloud platform for AI certification. While staying with one platform can deepen your expertise, switching platforms can expand your knowledge, especially for multi-cloud environments. Choose the platform most relevant to your business area.
How long is my AI certification valid?
expand accordion
The
AWS Certified AI Practitioner (AF-C01)
is valid for
3 years
.
The
Microsoft Azure AI Fundamentals (AI-900)
certification
does not expire
.
Tip:
Add your certification to
Percipio
to get automatic renewal reminders.
