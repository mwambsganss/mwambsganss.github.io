Lilly's AI Guidance
L
illy is dedicated to using AI responsibly and ethically. Before using AI in your work, please review Lilly’s AI guidance.
Using AI Responsibly at Lilly
As with all breakthrough technologies, there are risks and benefits to using AI. When used incorrectly, AI can produce biased or incorrect results.
Before using AI products, you must
read the procedure
and utilize the
guidelines
.
Read Procedure
Decision Tree for Submitting a Use Case to the AI Registry
Before using a new AI tool, please consult this decision tree to determine whether your use case requires a submission to the AI registry.
Try the Decision Tree
Guidelines for Use of AI-Generated Media (Images, Video, Audio)
When using AI to generate media (e.g., images, video, or audio) for your work at Lilly, you must follow guidance on the use and labeling of media. Make sure to read the guidance thoroughly before using AI-generated media.
Read the Guidance
Do
Do engage and ask questions when unsure of appropriate AI use.
Do verify outputs of AI for accuracy
Do know the data sources, including biases and limitations.
Do get help on AI contracts.
Don't
Don't use public AI tools outside of these guidelines to make logical decisions about low-risk use.
Don’t put in personal or confidential information unless approved by Lilly (e.g., Copilot with Bing is approved).
Don’t put intellectual property at risk.
Don’t enter into paid AI license agreements for company use.
