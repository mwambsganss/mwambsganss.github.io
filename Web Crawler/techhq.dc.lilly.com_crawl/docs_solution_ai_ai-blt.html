Template-driven Natural Language Generation (NLG) platform positioned for enterprise use in targeted functions. As of December 2022, documents in the Clinical and Finance functions (see use cases section) are available, with additional functions (e.g. Science, Marketing, etc.) to be built into the platform over the next 3 years of collaboration (through 2025).
